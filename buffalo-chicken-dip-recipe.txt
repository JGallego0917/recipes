Ingredients:
2 cups shredded cooked chicken 
1 (8 oz. pkg.) cream cheese, softened 
1/2 cup FRANK'S RedHot® Original Cayenne Pepper Sauce 
    or FRANK'S RedHot® Buffalo Wings Sauce 
1/2 cup Hidden Valley® Original Ranch® Dressing 
1/2 cup Treasure Cave ® Blue Cheese Crumbles 

Directions:
PREHEAT oven to 350°F.

COMBINE all ingredients and spoon into shallow 1-quart baking dish.

BAKE 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through; stir. Garnish 
with chopped green onions if desired and serve with crackers 
and/or vegetables.
